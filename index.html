Everyone has a
story to tell

Aarav was a web developer student. He loved learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Most of his days were spent building small websites, but deep inside, he sometimes felt lonely.

One day in the library, he met Meera, a girl struggling with her computer project. Aarav decided to help, and that moment changed everything.

They started studying together. When the code failed, they laughed. When it finally worked, they celebrated like it was a big victory. Slowly, their friendship grew into something more beautiful.

One evening, after finishing a project, Aarav said softly, “Meera, you are the best part of my life. Will you be with me always?”

Meera smiled and replied, “Yes, Aarav. Just like our code, we will fix errors and stay together forever.”

From that day on, Aarav knew the best creation of his life was not a website—it was his love story with Meera.
